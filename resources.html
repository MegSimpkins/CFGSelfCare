How are you feeling?

Hover your mouse over each emotion to recieve advice!

Sad

It's completely normal to feel sad every now and again. Try to figure out why you're feeling this way and target the issue head on. Why not try to be positive and write down a few things that you are grateful for, or are proud of.

Anxious

Breathe! It's okay to be anxious, especially in the middle of a global pandemic. Why not try some online meditation sessions to ease your mind. Talking to a friend or family member about what is worrying you may also help!

Lonely

Everyone feels lonely at some point in their lives. Try to reach out to a friend or relative who you can trust. If this isn't possible, perhaps consider joining a group or society, whether that face to face or online, so you can start building connections with others with a similar mindset to you.
