Hello and welcome to our website which we hope will help everyone navigate the complexities of self care, in a time when it is needed more tan ever.

What is self care?

Self-care techniques and general lifestyle changes can help manage the symptoms of many mental health problems. They may also help prevent some problems from developing or getting worse. This space on the web is a community space to share, tips and events.
