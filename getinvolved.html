Get Involved

We are living considerably faster paced lives than ever before, juggling careers, family and our social lives all while trying to eat healthily, exercise and get enough sleep feels almost impossible which leads to stress, anxiety and burn out. It is more important than ever before that we take time out of our day to look after our own well being and while that can be easier said than done, you cannot pour from an empty cup!

Below is a list of workshops available in Manchester, Birmingham and London which can help you to get started on your self care journey.
